# Alert Types Explained

This document explains **all alert types** generated by the ESP32 Crypto Alert Monitor.
Alerts are designed to signal **contextual market situations**, not buy/sell commands.

---

## General principles
- Alerts are **event-based**, not continuous
- Cooldowns prevent repeated notifications
- Most alerts depend on **relative movement**, not absolute price
- Alerts gain meaning when combined (confluence)

## Alert philosophy
Alerts are **contextual signals**, not instructions.

The most reliable situations occur when:
- Multiple alerts align
- Trend, volatility, and timeframe agree

Think in **clusters**, not isolated alerts.
